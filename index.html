Inter-Connect Impart Centre (ICIC) is a community driven, Non governmental organisation that believes in diversity and shared responsibility. And seek to resolve community challenges through youth engagement and empowerment.
Our mission is to advance the socio economic development of deprived communities with a focus on youth empowerment
Our vision to be a channel of peace when it comes to community conflict challenges through youth engagement/dialogue. As well as to empower the youth to propell the achievement of the SDGs in their respective communities.
Ultimately, we seek to develop innovative ideas and ways to engage the youth in tackling poverty, hunger, education, peace and climate change.
